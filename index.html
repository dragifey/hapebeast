FAQ

MOST ASKED QUESTIONS

When will HAPE launch?

Our target date is in December 2021. We haven’t announced anything more specific than that.

How can I get on the HAPELIST?

HAPELIST status is awarded by hand to outstanding members of the HAPE community. That might mean you produce some art, music, poetry, food or anything else that represents HAPE – but it certainly means that you’re part of our server and being respectful and warm to others.

There are effectively two roles for HAPELIST – HL and OG. Members with the OG role have been here from the start or done something exceptional and will be able to mint two HAPES at launch.

Then how do I get a HAPE?

First you have to wait until December. And you’ll need to be on our HAPELIST. There will be no public sale.

Is my OG / HL role permanent?

No. We reserve the right to change your role (and remove your wallet from the HAPELIST) for any reason. Practically speaking the following will certainly see your role removed / downgraded and may result in you being ejected from the server.

i) Being disrespectful to other members of the HAPE community
ii) Running multiple accounts on our server, particularly with the intent of subverting the process we’ve designed to get HAPES to as many different people as possible
iii) Selling, trading or buying accounts

It’s also possible we’ll move you from HL to OG or from OG to HL based on your activity (or otherwise).

If I do [x], will that get me HAPELISTED?

Again, no guarantees. There is no circumstance in which we will promise a HAPELIST space ahead of time. It’s also worth noting that we won’t award HAPELIST for anything that puts you at risk of harm or contravenes local law.

How can I trust this is fair?

Practically, here’s what happens. If a ROLE spots something or someone they think is worth HAPELISTING they share it in a private group. One person in the senior ROLE team is responsible for going through those proposals, checking and changing the status if they agree. On occasion Alphas will award the HL status on their own.

Is that fair? Perhaps not. We know it’s imperfect, at least, and there will always be people acting in bad faith to trick us. Many will succeed. There is no perfect solution. Grinding produces terrible content. Cheats (sometimes) win competitions. Art can be paid for. Gas wars destroy everyone. Raffles get botted. Everything gets botted.

We’ve looked into every option. Hand-selection takes time and is vulnerable to human error but it is our preferred route. If that makes you uncomfortable you should not be on the server trying to grind out a HAPE.
